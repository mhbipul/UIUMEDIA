Due to inclement weather, all classes of undergraduate & graduate programs scheduled to be held tomorrow on October 25, 2022 (Tuesday) are hereby cancelled. The schedule of Make-up Classes will be notified later.
NEW ID CARD WITH RFID IS REQUIRED TO ACCESS THROUGH THE ENTRANCE OF UIU
This is for information of all UIU students that UIU is going to introduce Access Control System at the UIU entrances. When the Access Control System becomes operational, all UIU students will need to have a UIU – ID with RFID (Radio Frequency ID) to enter into the UIU Campus. Those who have already received ID card with RFID are requested to provide the information of their ID card with RFID to the officials at the Information Booths for CITS Database.
